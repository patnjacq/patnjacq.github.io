Meridian Idaho Temple Spotlight

The Meridian Idaho Temple is the fifth temple built in Idaho. This multi-level temple is topped with a beautiful gold dome-like structure supporting a gold-leafed statue of the angel Moroni. A meetinghouse and utility building share the property.
