Your Home Away from Home

Find a comfy base where you will be steps from the temple with easy dining options, babysitting services and changing rooms to make your service in the temple as easy as being at home. The whole family will enjoy free WiFi, hot breakfast, and our indoor pool.

Make your event memorable. We supply easily customizable catering menus and a beautiful space for your wedding dinner, brunch or reception. We also provide kitchen access for food preparation upon request.

Family Search Center

Temple Inn and Suites provides a family search center that supports family history research and ordinance work. Family history consultants are available to help with research, family ordinance cards, and can help with your many questions. Whether you are visiting to participate in sealings, weddings, receptions, ordinance work or youth trips our specialists are here to help.
